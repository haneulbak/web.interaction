haneul bak



from a self-portrait series is she some body, 2017


I can never express myself perfectly. There are only better and worse versions of expressions, which I aim to create as accurate as possible. It took me long to realize the imperfect nature of self-expression, and even longer to accept it. But it was not until I began to appreciate such fate of creative process that I grew serious about creating art. As much as I don’t know about others, the world, and the universe, I don’t understand my self. Why would I ever expect to perfectly express my self when I don’t even understand it?

My work circulates around two major mysteries: the self and the universe. I contemplate on my relationship to my self, environment, and the world, & depict the reflections as films, videos, photographs, and installation.

A self is a universe. A universe consistently and persistently births and burns stars. I see my creative expressions as the lights from the births and deaths of stars. I aim to be an actively alive universe in this sea of universes.
