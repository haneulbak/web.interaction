dream-homeland




Concept Statement

I am telling a personal story about my other self and friends
in my fantastical home, which is made from memories and dreams.
This virtual environment will transgress and challenge
the conventional norms of a home by reimagining the physical space in terms of feelings,
with an emphasis on personal importance and abnormal physicality.

Project Mandate and Interaction Template

The purpose of this project is to communicate a personal perspective on home by creating my emotional home.
My other self and friends within the virtual space are granted transhuman abilities such as flying or complete honesty
that would otherwise be unavailable in the “real” world in order to disrupt the tiring limitations of human physicality and the superficiality of educated societal facades.
Additionally, the spatial aspects and the interactions contribute to the narrative in the way they invoke curiosity and confusion in order to have a memorable impact on the participant.
